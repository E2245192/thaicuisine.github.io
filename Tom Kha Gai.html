Tom Kha Gai


Tom Kha Gai is a classic Thai soup known for its rich, creamy, and aromatic coconut milk broth.
It usually features chicken as the main protein, along with a variety of herbs and spices. .

Ingredients

1 lb boneless, skinless chicken thighs, thinly sliced 4 cups chicken broth
1 can (13.5 oz) coconut milk
1 stalk lemongrass, bruised and chopped into 2-inch pieces
3-4 kaffir lime leaves, torn into pieces (substitute with lime zest if unavailable)
1-inch piece galangal or ginger, sliced
200g (about 1 cup) mushrooms, sliced
1 medium onion, thinly sliced
2-3 Thai bird's eye chilies, smashed (adjust according to spice preference)
2 tablespoons fish sauce
1 tablespoon soy sauce
1 tablespoon sugar
1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
Fresh cilantro leaves, for garnish
Lime wedges, for serving

Instructions

Prepare Ingredients:

Slice the chicken thinly. Bruise the lemongrass stalk with the back of a knife and chop it into 2-inch pieces.
Tear the kaffir lime leaves into pieces.

Make the Broth:

In a pot, combine chicken broth, coconut milk, lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves, and galangal or ginger.
Bring the mixture to a simmer over medium heat, then reduce the heat to low. Let it simmer for about 10-15 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse.

Add Chicken and Vegetables:

Add sliced chicken, mushrooms, sliced onion, and Thai bird's eye chilies to the pot.
Simmer until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.

Season the Soup:

Stir in fish sauce, soy sauce, and sugar. Adjust the seasoning to your taste.

Add Tomatoes and Garnish:

Add halved cherry tomatoes to the soup and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes. Remove the lemongrass pieces and galangal slices.
Garnish the soup with fresh cilantro leaves.
